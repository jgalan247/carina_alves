About Carina

Born and raised in Jersey with Portuguese heritage, I understand firsthand the challenges facing working families in our community.

Before entering politics, I spent years as a Mathematics teacher, specializing in ICT. I saw how housing issues, healthcare access, and financial pressures directly affected my students' ability to learn and thrive.

That's why I ran for office in 2018 ‚Äî and why I continue to fight for affordable housing, accessible healthcare, and transparent government that works for everyone, not just the privileged few.

I'm proud to represent St Helier Central and to be Jersey's first States Member of Portuguese heritage.
